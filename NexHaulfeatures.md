This is perhaps the strongest fit. Companies that own tanker fleets and distribute diesel or PMS to filling stations or industrial sites.

2. Large Construction & Infrastructure Firms
Firms managing massive projects (dams, highways, rail) that maintain their own internal fuel dumps and heavy machinery (excavators, dump trucks).

Why: They need to track fuel dispensed to their own equipment vs. what was loaded. The Community Provision feature is also common here, where they might provide water or fuel to local communities nearby as part of CSR.
